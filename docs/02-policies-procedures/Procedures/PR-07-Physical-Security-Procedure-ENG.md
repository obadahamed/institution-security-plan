## 1. Purpose

To prevent unauthorized physical access, intrusion, and vandalism, and to protect the organization's facilities and technological assets (such as servers and devices) from theft, damage, or environmental disasters.

---

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all bank devices and technological assets, including:

- Network devices (routers, switches, firewalls).

- Data centers and communications centers.

---

## 3. Detailed Steps

### Step 1 — Implementing Access Control

- Using magnetic ID cards or fingerprints for entry.

- Activating the Anti-passback system to prevent two people from entering with the same card.

### Step 2 — Implementing Visitor Management

- Registering visitors in a dedicated log at reception.

- Issuing a colored "Visitor" card, different from employee cards.

- Mandating the presence of an escort for visitors in sensitive areas (such as server rooms). ### Step 3 — Implementing Testing Phase

### Step 3 — Implementing Data Center Security:

- Locking server rooms with double or biometric locks.

- Installing fire, humidity, and heat sensors.

### Step 4 — Implementing CCTV Surveillance:

- Distributing cameras to cover all entrances, exits, and sensitive corridors.

- Ensure that recordings are of high quality and stored for at least 90 days.

### Step 6 — Verification

- Conduct periodic inspections after working hours to ensure that no confidential documents or flash drives are left on desks.

- Lock computer screens when leaving the office is mandatory.

---

## 4. Responsibilities

| Entity | Responsibility |
| ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Security and Safety Management** | Monitoring global security bulletins (such as CVE) is responsible for maintaining doors, locks, and cameras. |
| **IT Department** | Responsible for internal server room security and managing smart card access (who is authorized to open each door). |
| **All Employees** | Responsible for wearing ID badges at all times. |
