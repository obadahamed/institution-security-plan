## 1. Objective

To manage the risks associated with external vendors accessing the organization's data and systems, and to ensure the continuity of services while maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of information exchanged with them.

---

## 2. Scope

- All vendors, contractors, cloud service providers, and partners who have access to the organization's data or infrastructure.


---

## 3. Detailed Steps

### Step 1 — Conducting a Due Diligence Assessment Before Contracting:

- Sending a security questionnaire to the supplier to assess their level of protection (e.g., Do they have ISO 27001 certification? How do they protect their data?).

- Reviewing the supplier's market history and ensuring there are no previous breaches that have not been addressed.

### Step 2 — Implementing Contractual Requirements:

- Including an "Information Security Appendix" in the contract that obligates the supplier to adhere to the organization's standards.

- Requiring the supplier to notify the organization immediately (within 24 hours, for example) in the event of a security breach that could affect its data.

### Step 3 — Implementing Remote Access Control:

- Granting the supplier "temporary" access only when needed (Just-in-Time Access).

- Monitoring and recording all activities performed by the supplier within the organization's systems (Session Recording).

### Step 4 — Conducting the Annual Audit:

- Conducting site visits or desk audits to ensure the supplier remains compliant with the agreed security terms.

### Step 5 — Offboarding:

- Withdrawing all permissions immediately upon contract expiration.

- Ensuring the supplier deletes or restores all existing organizational data and signs a declaration to that effect.

---

## 4. Responsibilities

| Entity | Responsibility |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **IT Department:** | Creating supplier accounts and activating secure access technologies (such as VPN and MFA). |
| **Cybersecurity Team:** | Assessing the supplier's security risks and approving (or rejecting) technical engagement with them. |
| **Procurement/Contracts Department:** | Coordinating between the supplier and technical departments and ensuring Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) are signed. |

---

## 5. Required Documents and Records

| Document | Responsible Party for Storage |
| ------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Security Assessment Reports | Information Security |
| Vendor Inventory | IT Team |
| Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) | Legal Department |

---

## 6. Review

- This procedure is reviewed **annually** or immediately following any security incident related to human error.

- Any modification requires the approval of the Information Security Manager and documentation in the Change Log.
