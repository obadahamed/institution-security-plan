## 1. Objective

To protect lives, minimize material losses, and ensure the continuity of the organization's vital operations in the event of a disaster (natural, technological, or security-related) and to recover as quickly as possible.

---

## 2. Scope

All of the organization's facilities, personnel, technological systems, and core operational processes. This also includes the suppliers and partners the organization relies on to maintain its operations.

---

## 3. Detailed Steps

### Step 1 — **Activate the Emergency Plan:**

- Define the "Groundbreaking": When do we consider an incident a disaster? (e.g., a main server outage for more than two hours or a fire).

- Activate the group alert system to notify relevant employees and teams.

### Step 2 — **Conduct Field Crisis Management:**

- Evacuate the premises (in cases of physical disasters) and proceed to the assembly point.

- Activate the "Leadership Team" to make decisions regarding business continuity or temporary closure.

### Step 3 — **Conduct Disaster Recovery (DRP):**

- Begin restoring systems from backups on the alternative site or in the cloud.

- Activate alternative internet and communication lines to ensure team continuity.

### Step 4 — **Conduct Work from Alternative Locations:** - Activate the comprehensive "Remote Work" protocol for all critical employees.

- Ensure staff have access to essential tools (email, financial systems) remotely.

### Step 5 — **Restoration Procedure:**

- Assess the damage at the original site before returning.

- Gradually transfer data and operations from the backup site back to the original site after repairs.

---

## 4. Responsibilities

| Entity | Responsibility |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Crisis Management Committee:** | Making sovereign and financial decisions during the crisis. |
| **Technical Recovery Team:** | Systems and network engineers responsible for data recovery and operating backup servers. |
| **Staff:** | Adhere to safety instructions, keep emergency contact numbers, and be prepared to work from home when required. |
