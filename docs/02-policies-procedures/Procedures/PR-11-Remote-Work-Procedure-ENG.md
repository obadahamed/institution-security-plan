## 1. Purpose

To define the security procedures necessary to protect the organization's data when accessed via mobile devices (phones, tablets, laptops) or through working from locations outside the organization's premises, in order to reduce the risk of breaches or data loss.

---

## 2. Scope

This procedure includes all employees and contractors who use:

- Organizational mobile devices.

- Personal devices to access work data (Bring Your Own Device - BYOD policy).

- Connecting to the network from homes, cafes, or while traveling.

---

## 3. Detailed Steps

### Step 1 — Implementing Approval and Authorization:

Data is divided into clear levels, typically:

- Remote work is only permitted after signing a formal "Remote Work Agreement."

- Job and employee eligibility is assessed (Does their job require access to highly sensitive data that cannot be removed?).

### Step 2 — Implementing Secure Connection (VPN & MFA):

- The use of the organization's Virtual Private Network (VPN) is mandatory for accessing any internal systems.

- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is enforced for every remote login.

### Step 3 — Implementing Endpoint Security:

- The use of managed devices is preferred. If using a personal device (BYOD), approved antivirus software must be installed and the system updated.

- The use of open public Wi-Fi networks (such as those in cafes) is prohibited unless an encrypted VPN is used. Step 4 — Physical and Perimeter Security

- Require employees to lock their screens when moving away from the device.

- Ensure that no unauthorized individuals (including family members) are viewing sensitive data on the screen.

Step 5 — Conducting Virtual Meetings

- Use only approved platforms (such as Teams or Zoom Pro) and set a password for each meeting and a waiting room.

---

4. Responsibilities

| Entity | Responsibility |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Direct Supervisor:** | Monitor employee productivity and ensure adherence to working hours and effective communication. |
| **Cybersecurity Team:** | Monitor login logs from outside the network to detect any suspicious activity from unfamiliar countries or websites. |
| **Employees:** | Full responsibility for the security of the device entrusted to them and its protection against theft or loss. |
