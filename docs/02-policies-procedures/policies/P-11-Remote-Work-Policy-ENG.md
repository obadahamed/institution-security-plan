## 1. Purpose

To define the necessary security controls to protect the organization's data when accessed via mobile devices (phones, tablets, laptops) or through working from locations outside the organization's premises, in order to reduce the risk of data breaches or loss.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy covers all employees and contractors who use:

- Organizational mobile devices.

- Personal devices to access work data (Bring Your Own Device - BYOD policy).

- Connecting to the network from homes, cafes, or while traveling.

---

## 3. Policy Provisions

### - **Authentication and Encryption:** All devices must be protected with strong passwords or biometric authentication, and full disk encryption must be enabled.

### - **Secure Connection:** Accessing the organization's systems via public Wi-Fi networks without using a VPN approved by the organization is prohibited.

### - **Device Management (MDM):** The organization has the right to install mobile device management software to control access and separate work data from personal data.

### - **System Updates:** Ensure that the device's operating system and security applications are updated regularly.

### - **Remote Wipe:** The "Remote Wipe" feature must be enabled to erase work data in case of loss or theft of a device.

In addition:

- Ensure the screen is not visible to others in public places.

- Printing confidential documents is prohibited in unsecured remote work locations.

- The home router must be secured with a strong password and kept up to date.

---

## 4. Responsibilities

| Entity | Responsibility |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Employee | Responsible for the physical safety of the device, preventing family members or friends from using it, and reporting its loss immediately (within one hour).
| IT Department | Responsible for providing secure communication channels (VPNs), offering technical support for mobile devices, and ensuring they meet security specifications.
| Information Security Department | Monitors remote access logs to detect any suspicious login attempts.


---

## 5. Penalties

- Using rooted/jailbroken devices to access data is a serious violation that will result in suspension of privileges.

- Negligence in securing a device that leads to the leakage of organizational data exposes the employee to legal accountability and strict disciplinary measures.

--

## 6. Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or whenever new communication technologies emerge to ensure comprehensive security coverage.
