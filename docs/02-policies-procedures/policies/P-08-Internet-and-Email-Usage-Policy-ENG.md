---

## 1. Purpose

To define acceptable and prohibited use of the organization's internet and email services, ensuring network protection from malware, preventing data leaks, and maintaining employee productivity.

---

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees and contractors using the organization's technology resources, whether on-site or remotely.

---

## 3. Terms and Conditions

- ### **Professional Use:** Email and internet access are for work purposes only. Limited personal use is permitted, provided it does not impact productivity or excessively consume network resources.

### - **Prohibited Content:** Visiting websites containing illegal material is strictly prohibited.

### - **Email Security:** Opening attachments or clicking on links in unsolicited emails is prohibited (to avoid phishing). Using your official email address to register on non-work-related websites is also prohibited.

### - **Confidentiality:** Sending sensitive or confidential data (such as customer data or trade secrets) via email without encryption or using authorized channels is prohibited.

### - **Impersonation:** Sending emails intended to mislead or impersonate the organization without authorization is prohibited.

---

## 4. Responsibilities

| Entity | Responsibility |

| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| IT Department | Responsible for web filtering, monitoring data flow to detect threats, and updating antivirus systems. |
| Management | Monitor usage reports in case of suspected resource misuse. |
|Employees | Responsible for the content of messages sent from their accounts and for immediately reporting any suspicious messages received in their inbox. |


---

## 5. Penalties

Violating this policy will result in:

- Internet access being blocked or email privileges being restricted.

- Formal disciplinary action, which may include a warning or termination in cases of leaking sensitive data or using the internet for illegal activities.

---

## 6. Review

This policy will be reviewed annually to reflect developments in social media tools and new cyber threats.
