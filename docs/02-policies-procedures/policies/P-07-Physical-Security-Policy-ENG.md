## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to prevent unauthorized physical access, intrusion, and vandalism, and to protect the organization's facilities and technological assets (such as servers and devices) from theft, damage, or environmental disasters.

---

## 2. Scope
This policy covers all of the organization's geographic locations and buildings, including:

- Administrative offices and reception areas.

- Data centers and communications centers.

- Areas where sensitive data and documents are stored.

- - Parking and External Entrances

---

## 3. Terms and Conditions

### 3.1 Defining Security Zones

The building is divided into zones (public, restricted, and highly sensitive). Access to sensitive zones is restricted to authorized personnel only.

### 3.2 Access Control Systems

Smart cards, biometric fingerprinting, or electronic keys are used for entry and exit.

### 3.3 Visitor Policy

All visitors must register their details upon reception, wear a clearly visible "Visitor" badge, and be accompanied at all times while in non-public areas.

---

## 4. Responsibilities

| Department | Responsibility |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Security and Safety Department | Responsible for guarding entrances, maintaining cameras, and issuing access cards. |
| Information Technology Department | Responsible for securing server rooms and ensuring the proper functioning of fire and cooling systems within them. |
| Staff | Responsible for wearing official identification badges, preventing unauthorized individuals from following them (tailgating), and reporting anyone not wearing an identification badge. |


---

## 5. Penalties

- Tampering with security systems (such as disabling cameras or leaving security doors open) is a serious violation.

- Any loss of organizational assets due to negligence in following physical security rules will result in disciplinary action ranging from a warning to termination of employment, with the responsible employee being held liable for the value of the lost assets.

---

## 6. Review

This policy is reviewed annually, and a periodic **field audit** (every 6 months) is conducted to ensure the integrity of locks, cameras, and fire alarm systems.
