## 1. Purpose

This policy aims to enhance the security of digital accounts and protect the organization's sensitive data from unauthorized access. It also aims to protect against brute-force attacks.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all Al Noor Bank employees across all departments and job levels, including new employees and external contractors with access to the bank's systems.

---

## 3. Terms and Conditions

### 3.1 **Mandatory:**
- Creating a strong password of at least 10 uppercase and lowercase letters, a symbol, and at least one number is a prerequisite for opening any digital account with the bank.

### 3.2 Approved Methods
- The use of password generator applications is permitted.

### 3.3 Password Confidentiality
- Sharing any password with anyone is strictly prohibited.

### 3.4 Password Change
- Employees are required to change their passwords at specified intervals.

---

## 4. Responsibilities

| Entity | Responsibility |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **IT/Security Department** | Responsible for providing the necessary tools, configuring technical settings, and providing technical support to users. |
| **Managers** | Ensuring their teams adhere to the policy and do not exceed its limits. |
| **All Employees** | Responsible for setting passwords for their accounts |


---

## 5. Penalties

Violating or failing to comply with this policy will result in the following actions, depending on the severity of the violation:

1- Immediate suspension of system access privileges until the situation is rectified.

2- Disciplinary action in accordance with the organization's Human Resources regulations.

3- In cases of gross negligence leading to data leaks, the penalty may be dismissal or legal prosecution.

---

## 6. Review

- This policy will be reviewed **annually** by the Information Security Manager.

- It will also be reviewed immediately after any security incident.
